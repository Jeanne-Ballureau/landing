What is HopeTrips

HopeTrips is a children charity created by 5 friends in 2015, who believed that every children should have the same opportunities in life. The association organizes humanitarian trips, from 2 weeks to 3 months, and go to countries to distribute food, clothes, toys and books to children, but mostly to spend time with them. If you want to participate in our journey and put a smile on children faces, feel free to submit your application.
